About Me, The Artist !

Hi! My name is Arta Mekuli. I'm a first generation student / artist based in New York City. I am currently studying at Fordham University for my BA in Political Science with a Minor in Computer Science. I specialize in creating streaming assets and content for various Content Creators and VTuber's in the online space (creators and brands such as SkittyKat, SalteaKichee, VShojo, etc). In my spare time I like to crochet, sew, 3D model, amongst several other hobbies! When I'm not in school, I work with children with disabilities and do freelance illustration.

I mainly specialize in graphic illustrations and branding. Please check out more of my work on my social media pages @ greybenjii!

Art Programs and Materials I use: iPad air 10.6 + Procreate AND XP Pen Artist 15.6 pro + Clip Studio Paint.

Thank you, Arta <3
